MISSION

The Mission of Nunukragang project is to bring together passionate people to collaborate and to build an online dictionary for indigenous language in Borneo.

VISION

Our vision is to produce digital materials on indigenous language accessable to everyone anywhere in the world.

LANGUAGE MODERATORS

We need language moderators to monitor, edit and make sure the word meaning is correct. Must be able to speak the language to become a moderator.

VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

Volunteering programme is open to anyone regardless of race and background. You don't have to know any indigenous sabahan language to be part of this project.

VILLAGE TRIP

We will be organizing regular weekend trips to villages around Sabah for word hunting while spending time to learn not only the language but the true Sabah indigenous culture.

PROJECT ROADMAP

  • Oct 2017 - Basic feature to key in words
